How to Grill a Juicy Steak

Grilling a juicy steak begins with choosing the right cut and marinating it for flavor. Season the steak generously with salt, pepper, and your favorite spices. Preheat the grill to high heat and oil the grates. Place the steak on the grill and cook for a few minutes on each side, depending on your preferred level of doneness. Use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before slicing to retain its juices.
